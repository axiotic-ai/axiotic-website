# Strategy for Targeted Client Pages

To better convert specific buyer personas, we plan to introduce targeted landing pages that speak directly to distinct "Jobs to be Done."

## 1. /audit (Risk & Governance Focus)
*   **Target Audience:** CTOs, CIOs, Legal/Compliance Officers at regulated enterprises.
*   **Core Message:** "De-risk your AI adoption before you build."
*   **Key Components:**
    *   Focus on the **AI Governance Audit**: compliance (EU AI Act), data privacy, ethical risk mapping.
    *   Case studies of avoiding costly mistakes.
    *   CTA: "Book a Risk Assessment."

## 2. /build (Engineering & Systems Focus)
*   **Target Audience:** Product Managers, VPs of Engineering, Founders who need to ship.
*   **Core Message:** "White-glove AI systems that actually work in production."
*   **Key Components:**
    *   Focus on **Models, Data & Eval Loops**: RAG pipelines, efficient small models, custom architectures.
    *   "No Science Projects" guarantee.
    *   Technical details on our stack (CPU-first options, latency optimization).
    *   CTA: "Discuss Your Build."

## 3. /team-building (Hiring & Org Design Focus)
*   **Target Audience:** Founders, Heads of Talent, Leaders building their first AI unit.
*   **Core Message:** "Don't hire a trophy team. Build a shipping team."
*   **Key Components:**
    *   **Interim Head-of-AI** service.
    *   Role design & candidate vetting (we filter the noise).
    *   Culture & retention strategy.
    *   CTA: "Get Hiring Support."

## 4. /upskilling (Education Focus)
*   **Target Audience:** L&D Managers, Engineering Directors with legacy teams.
*   **Core Message:** "Turn your engineers into AI practitioners."
*   **Key Components:**
    *   **Executive Workshops** (Strategy & Physics of AI).
    *   **Deep-Dive Bootcamps** (Hands-on engineering).
    *   Pedigree of our instructors (University Lecturers).
    *   CTA: "View Course Catalog."
